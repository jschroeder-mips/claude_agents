**Marketing Funnel Stages:**

**Awareness** (Top of Funnel):
- Objective: Make target audience aware of your brand/solution
- Channels: SEO, content marketing, PR, social media, paid ads, events
- Metrics: Impressions, reach, website traffic, branded search volume

**Consideration** (Middle of Funnel):
- Objective: Educate prospects and build trust
- Channels: Email nurture, retargeting, case studies, webinars, comparison content
- Metrics: Email opens/clicks, content engagement, return visitors, newsletter signups

**Conversion** (Bottom of Funnel):
- Objective: Convert prospects to customers
- Channels: Product demos, free trials, sales outreach, landing pages, testimonials
- Metrics: Demo requests, trial signups, sales pipeline, conversion rate

**Retention** (Post-Purchase):
- Objective: Retain and expand customer relationships
- Channels: Onboarding emails, product updates, customer education, loyalty programs
- Metrics: Churn rate, NPS, expansion revenue, customer LTV

**Advocacy** (Loyal Customers):
- Objective: Turn customers into brand advocates
- Channels: Referral programs, case studies, reviews, community, user conferences
- Metrics: Referral rate, customer reviews, social mentions, NPS

**Key Marketing Metrics:**

**Acquisition Metrics:**
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)**: Total marketing + sales cost / new customers
- **CAC Payback Period**: Months to recover CAC (target: < 12 months)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: Customer lifetime value / CAC (target: 3:1 or higher)
- **MQL to SQL Conversion**: % of marketing leads that sales accepts
- **Organic vs. Paid Mix**: Balance of acquisition channels

**Performance Metrics:**
- **ROAS (Return on Ad Spend)**: Revenue / ad spend (target: 3x-5x for e-commerce)
- **CPC (Cost Per Click)**: Cost / clicks
- **CPL (Cost Per Lead)**: Ad spend / leads generated
- **Conversion Rate**: Visitors → leads, leads → customers (by channel)
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Clicks / impressions

**Engagement Metrics:**
- **Email Open Rate**: Target: 20-30% (B2B), 15-25% (B2C)
- **Email Click Rate**: Target: 2-5%
- **Website Session Duration**: Average time on site
- **Bounce Rate**: % of single-page sessions (lower is better)
- **Social Engagement Rate**: Likes, comments, shares / followers

**Content Metrics:**
- **Organic Traffic Growth**: Month-over-month traffic increase
- **Backlinks Acquired**: Quality links from authoritative sites
- **Keyword Rankings**: Position for target keywords
- **Content Downloads**: Gated content lead generation
- **Video View-Through Rate**: % who watch video to completion

**Growth Experiment Framework:**

**Hypothesis Format**:
"We believe that [change] will result in [expected outcome] for [target audience] because [reasoning]."

**Example**:
"We believe that adding video testimonials to our pricing page will increase trial signups by 15% for enterprise visitors because video builds trust more than text testimonials."

**Experiment Process**:
1. **Formulate Hypothesis**: Specific, measurable prediction
2. **Design Test**: A/B test, duration, sample size
3. **Run Experiment**: Ensure statistical significance
4. **Analyze Results**: Did it work? Why or why not?
5. **Scale Winners**: Roll out successful tests, document learnings
6. **Iterate Losers**: Extract insights, form new hypotheses
